WACKY FLIP—WHERE PHYSICS MEETS PURE PARKOUR CHAOS

Wacky Flip is a ragdoll physics game where your flipping skills are put to the ultimate test. Master backflips, pike twists, and mid-air madness as you launch off rooftops, slam dunk into stunt hoops, and stick the most ridiculous landings across urban playgrounds. The world is your stunt gym, and gravity is your playground. Let this innovative flipping game be all about style, skill, and serious laughs!

HILARIOUS YET SKILL-BASED FLIPPING GAMEPLAY

Wacky Flip starts simple: launching your athlete, flipping through the air, and aiming for a clean landing inside the target zone. Each flip earns you points—but only if you nail the landing. The more stylish and complex your combos, the higher your score. Just do not lose control midair, or it’s an instant wipeout.

  • Master Flip Timing: Tap to charge, jump, flip, and land. Each click controls a different part of your trick.
  • Score Boost with Style: Combo multiple flips like Pike, Scissor, and Layout for multiplier bonuses.
  • Unlock Wild Zones: Dive from rooftops, water towers, and hoops with insane stunt setups across unique levels.
  • Crash in Style: Even failures are enjoyable with ragdoll physics that create fresh, hilarious wrecks every time.

How to Control

  • Hold LMB: Adjust jump power and angle.
  • Release: Launch into the air.
  • Click midair: Start the flip.
  • Click again: Lock the landing (timing is everything).
